In industrial and automotive markets, brand is often treated as a cost. That framing badly undersells what brand actually does.

## Where brand trust pays off

- **Pricing power** — defend margin instead of discounting.
- **Channel loyalty** — dealers favour brands that protect their reputation.
- **Faster expansion** — a known name opens doors.
- **Resilience** — trust carries a brand through supply shocks.

> Brand is not what you say about yourself. It is what the market can count on.
