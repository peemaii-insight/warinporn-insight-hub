Ask ten counter staff to explain the difference between AGM and EFB and you will likely get ten different answers. That confusion is a commercial problem.

## The practical difference

EFB is an upgraded flooded battery for entry-level start-stop. AGM goes further — supporting deeper cycling, higher charge acceptance, and the heavier loads of advanced start-stop, regenerative braking, and hybrid systems.

- **EFB** — basic start-stop and higher-than-standard demand.
- **AGM** — advanced start-stop, regenerative braking, high-accessory or hybrid vehicles.
- **Never downgrade** — a vehicle specified for AGM should never get EFB or conventional.

> Technology fit is not a technical detail — it is the foundation of repeat business.
