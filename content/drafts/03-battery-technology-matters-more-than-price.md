In a price-led market, the cheapest battery on the shelf wins the sale and loses the customer.

## The hidden cost of the cheapest option

A battery underspecified for a modern vehicle does not fail politely. It fails early, and the customer remembers the brand — not the discount.

> Price wins the transaction. Technology and trust win the relationship.
